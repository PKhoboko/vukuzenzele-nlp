Ukufaka Iinlilo zeGEPF Okwenziwe Lula

vukuzenzele unnamed

 Amalunga, abase benzi esele bathe the umhlalaphasi kunye nabazuzi besiKhwa ma somHlalaphasi sabaSe benzi bakaRhulumende (i-GEPF) kwanje sebano bubhadekelo nayibe aba kaneliseki ngendlela be nzelwa ngayo izinto.  Ukuqinisekisa ukuthi amalunga we-GEPF nemi ndeni yawo baphatheka kuhle, mhlapha ibhodi ivule i-ofisi esebenza ngamacala wemibango. UmBekilihlo  womHlalaphasi wabaSe benzi bakaRhulumende (i-GEPO) yi-ofisi yange khaya kodwana ezijame leko ye-GEPF. I-GEPF ingesinye see nkhwama zomhlalaphasi ezikulu khulu ephasini; inamalunga ayi-1.2 yee ngidi kunye nabantu esele bathethe umhlalaphasi nabazuzi abangaphezulu kwee-450 000. Isikhwamesi sasungulwa ngomThetho we-GEPF begodu silawulwa ngomthetho lo.

 U-Advocate Makhado Ramabulana uqatjhelwe isikhundla sokuba mBekili hlo. Uthi i-ofisi le yavulelwa ukusiza abantu abaneenlilo ngomsebenzi we-GEPF.
 I-ofisi le izakuba lisizo khulu ebantwini abanga namali yokubhadela ama gcwetha. Ngaphambi koba na abantu base imila ndwabo yemibango e-Ofi sini yomVikeli womPha kathi namkha emakhotho, bangakhombela uku lanyulelwa mBekilihlo.UmBekilihlo uzakulamula eendabeni ezenza kuri -yadeke ngokunganasizathu ukusetjenzwa kwembadalo ekufuze ibhadelwe aba yitlemileko. Kodwana-ke ingalamula imibango  engasingaphezulu kwe minyaka emithathu yenze kile.
 Ezinye iindaba eziwela ngaphasi kwe-GEPO ngilezi ezilandelako:
• Ukubhalelwa kweempha thimandla kukwenza u msebenzazo ngokomthe tho nangokwemithetjhwa na yesiKhwama; • Ukwephula isibopho nga phandle kwesizathu esi zwakalako; kunye • nemininingwana/nelwa zi elidurhisako elivezwe basebenzi be-GEPF.
Ngaphambi kokufaka isililo ku-GEPO, umuntu kufuze athumele isililo esitloli -weko ku-GEPF, ePhikweni leLawulo lemiHlalaphasi ka Rhulumende (i-GPAA) namkha kumqatjhi walezi injamiso, abanikele ithuba lokulungisa indaba yesili lweso.“Kufuze ubanikele ama langa ama-30 ukulungisa lokho okubonileko okunga kalungi. Nayibe kudlula amalanga ama-30 isililo sakho singakararululwa, unga sithumela kumBekilihlo,” kukhlathulula u-Ramabulana.  UmBekilihlo angeze avume ukusithatha isililo nayibe sekuyaphenywa ngesililweso engcenye. “Kungakapheli iimveke ezimbili ukusukela ngelanga isililo esithunyelwe ngalo, i-GEPO izakuqala isililweso bese ibikela umfakisililo ukuthi ingakghona namkha angeze ikghone na uku phenya.”
  Nayibe umBekilihlo uya bhala ukuphenya ngesililo esithileko, umfakisililo angatlolela i-ofisi ye-GEPO kungakapheli ama-21 wama langa aveze iinzathu zokuthi kubayini akholwa ukuthi isililweso singomunye wemi sebenzi ethunywe i-GEPO.
